Site Name: SP Weekly Chamber

The site has this name because it will provide a weekly update on the city's events and commerce.

Purpose

Announce local events and commerces weekly (especially new ones), keeping updated and arousing the interest of the population and mainly helping local businesses and events to spread.

Scenarios

• How can I search for a new in a specific date?
If you want to search an older news/event you can search in the news/events page.

• How often are the news updated?
All news and events are updated weekly, but some events can stay in out home page for an entire month.

• What type of event/commerce do you focus on?
New commerces and big events in the city.
